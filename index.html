System Features

R33 engages in trading with up to 33 different pairs using the 1% liquidity pool of PEPE-LP (uni-v2) on swap.ogpepe.io. This strategy aids in the liquidity and stability of R33 and other projects, potentially increasing the overall portfolio value.

Fee Structure

Includes a 1% pool fee for long-term liquidity and auto-compounding, and a 3% feeCollector for extracting value from the system.

Token Introduction to Multiple Pools

Introducing Token X into various liquidity pools on different exchanges or DeFi platforms, paired with assets like USDT, ETH, etc., creates a diverse ecosystem crucial for arbitrage opportunities.

Monitoring Price Differences

Monitoring the price of Token X across pools is essential. Arbitrage opportunities arise from noticeable price differences between pools.

Executing Arbitrage Trades

Arbitrageurs or bots buy Token X in cheaper pools and sell in higher-priced pools. The profit is the price difference minus transaction and transfer fees.

3% Transfer Fee

A 3% fee is applied to every transfer, collected by the FeeCollector on all transactions.

Arbitrage Bots and Fee Calculation

Arbitrage bots exploit price differences across pools, incurring standard liquidity pool fees plus the 3% transfer fee.

Profitability Calculation for Arbitrage Bots

For profitability, the price difference must exceed 5% (2% in trading fees and 3% in transfer fees, which are kept by the R33 ecosystem)
